Introducing my classic Belgian waiter's apron (fond memories of hot chocolate and waffles in the Grand Place, Brussels).

We were asked to design an apron for a new restaurant in Los Angeles. We supplied 100 natural Orkney L'Aprons™ and they added the embroidered logo. Now, you can wear the same European apron yourself — pas de logo — je m'excuse.

I have given it two capacious pockets divided by a sleek pencil slot. So professional.

34" wide by 27" deep, the new apron comes in natural, white, charcoal, indigo and black Orkney linen and has the traditional long strings for you to tie in front — just remember to fold and tie them together in a loose loop when you wash it.

L'Apron™ is priced at just $50. Such good value you can afford to buy all the colors!
