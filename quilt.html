This is a large quilt with three colours, Indigo, White and Natural. The tufting holding it together is indigo thread, the lining is natural smooth linen and the fill is cotton. There is a tag with my initials as maker.

This throw contains Charcoal, Black, White, Natural, and a flash of red. The tufting is also red, on a pieced natural linen lining with cotton fill. There is a tag with my initials as maker.

The excitement of making a quilt the proper way, from scraps, is the balance between accuracy and ingenuity. The aim is to create beauty of expression with economy – even frugality – of means.

I was inspired by the patterns and creativity of Amish and Gees Bend Quilt makers.

A lot of work goes into making these quilts – no two are ever the same.

The charm of making these quilts is the fine mix of putting it together and the simplicity of the material.

It really please me to be making something using scraps which would be otherwise thrown away – and by dint of skill and effort to make something beautiful, something collectable.

Apart from these two quilts and a third which is underway, I plan to make any other quilts on demand – the size and colors you want – assuming, of course I have the linen scraps.

So I am making these future quilts as custom quilts – contact me at tricia@roughlinen.com or by phone (415) 235 6231 and tell me what you would like, both size and color way – the squares are 20” – so the size needs to be in 20” increments, I can make any size.
