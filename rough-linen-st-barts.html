st barts linen

St Barts 100% linen is middleweight and comes in (pale) blue, mix (natural and white interwoven), white and various unpredictable colors, so I use it mostly for custom work, in particular the broadly striped, subtly nautical curtains and duvets which have proved such a hit. Let me know colors you want to see and I will send you samples - because it changes all the time.
