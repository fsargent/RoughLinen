My Rough Linen sheets, duvet cover and pillow slips in the Glass House


A request from the folk who run Philip Johnson's Glass House, "could they use my bed linen?" In the Glass House? THE Glass House? Oh, yes! I told my good friends at Remodelista, who in turn contacted the Glass House for permission to do a photo shoot. One day in June, 2014, Margot Guralnick and I arrived on site with Matthew Williams, a professional stills photographer. I took my video camera and shot away myself. Margot's Remodelista article is here.
