St Barts curtains can come plain, or striped—here are two combinations: blue and white, and mix and white stripes.

Our plain version has a 2” casing at the top, 5” closed hem at the bottom. Stripes have no casing or hem.

I always recommend ring-clips – not only do they draw more easily, you can adjust length and create inverted pleats, even hang a blackout layer without interfering with the flow of the linen. Easiest way to choose ring clips is to Google ‘drapery ring clips”, and then click Images. No wading through ads or descriptions, just useful pictures.

Custom sizing is possible, email me for a quote.
