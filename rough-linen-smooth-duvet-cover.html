When you want lighter duvet covers, choose my double-width sheeting with its finer weave, cool ‘hand’ and smooth finish. This is the same fabric I use for my sheets and pillow slips. I call it Smooth.

Natural is a soft taupe, straight from the field. White has been yarn-bleached for strength, without optical brighteners, so it comes to you a soft off-white, and will lighten with washing and use.

My close-woven, specially woven sheeting is water-finished, no need to wash before use. Allowance is made for shrinkage.

There is a neat concealed button closure along the bottom edge.

I also make duvet covers in Orkney and St Barts linen.
