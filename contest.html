Rough & Smooth

Rough Linen Instagram Photo Contest 2016

Prizes:

(1) Grand Prize: $250 Rough Linen gift certificate

(2) Runner up prizes: $50 Rough Linen certificate

Eligibility:

Play a little! Show us your interpretation of Rough and Smooth in everyday life. You can be as literal as you please (an unfortunate accident with your linens) or be creative (smooth sheets in rough weather conditions). Let your imagination go wild!

  • Must have at least one Rough Linen product in the photo
  • Must have hashtags #roughlinen #roughandsmooth2016
  • Must be submitted between 1/30/2016 and 2/13/2016
  • Make sure we can view it! (Private profiles won't be visible to us.)

Judging:

We will select the photo which tickles us most, is most creative and memorable to win the grand prize.

*All judging decisions are final. Please do not contact us about the status of entries or judging.

Rough Linen will notify the winner via the Instagram profile provided with hashtag.

We won’t accept entries that contain anything:

That violates any person’s rights, including but not limited to copyright.

Are sexually explicit, rude, obscene, violent or inappropriate.

That put any person or animal in danger.
