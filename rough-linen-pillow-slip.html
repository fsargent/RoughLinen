Pillow slips cover the pillow you sleep on, so fine weave and smoothness are important.

Simple linen pillow slips are as plain as can be, with a 2" hem.

Oxford linen pillow slips are more formal, with a 2" flange all round.

All have a European-style deep tuck to hold the pillow inserts securely inside so they don't show.

Pillow slips come in one fabric (Smooth) ands two colors. Natural is a soft taupe, straight from the field. White has been yarn-bleached for strength, without optical brighteners, so it comes to you a soft off-white, and will lighten with washing and use.

My close-woven, commissioned sheeting is water-finished, no need to wash before use. Allowance is made for shrinkage.
