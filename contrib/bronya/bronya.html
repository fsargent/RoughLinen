I like to think of my Berkeley, California home as a combination of Tuscan inn and Deco-era Mexican hacienda (if there were such a thing). I've filled it with treasures I have collected from my travels worldwide, but also throughout Northern California.
I have found that over time I have never regretted the investment that I've made in high-quality bedding.
