Shams come in all the Orkney colors (natural, white, black, charcoal) or in the lighter-weight St Barts (blue, mix, and white) fabric. You also have a choice of perfectly plain two-hole buttons in white, black, or (for $4 extra) the subtle natural texture of coconut. There is an invisible zipper option for minimalists. (I can’t show you a photo. It’s invisible).

Shams are best 2” smaller than their infill, so they look plump and full (watch my video Choosing Infills). Pillowslips on the other hand are loose, for comfort.
